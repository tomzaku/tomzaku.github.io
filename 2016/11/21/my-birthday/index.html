My story:

Why I make this blog? I want to keep my emotion, feeling my trip in Blog. And it’s memorable, and

20 November, 2015.
My Unbelievable birthday ever.

Yesterday, I decided traveling to Lao Bao from Hue city. There’re 2 way from Hue City to Lao Bao:
One is QL1A street and the rest is HCM street. So I make the rule “Don’t go the same way”. That mean morning i came at QL1A street and came back at HCM street.
The story is not easy in this way. I started at 9:00 am from Hue city with a niece (3 years old), a sister-in-law, a friend. We have 4 people traveling to Lao Bao. Like I said, I bike at QL1A first, It’s so easy to handling. The street is a good and wide. The sky is so clear, and sunny day. I finished Lao Bao city at 1pm. I have luch at 12pm. It far from Lao Bao 11 km. So we decided go market until 2:30 pm. And we come back home. But I decided go another way. HCM Street, I want to discover the scenery and people live here. But I don’t care it’s bad choose when I selected.
